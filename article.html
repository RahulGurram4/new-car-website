10 things to check before buying your next car

A simple checklist that helps you compare options beyond the sticker price.

  1. Total budget: include taxes, insurance, registration, and maintenance.
  2. Vehicle history: ask for service records and ownership information.
  3. Test drive quality: assess braking, steering, visibility, and cabin noise.
  4. Safety features: compare airbags, driver assistance, and camera coverage.
  5. Fuel or charging needs: match the powertrain to your driving routine.
  6. Warranty coverage: understand what is included and for how long.
  7. Technology usability: infotainment should be easy to use while driving.
  8. Cargo and comfort: check trunk space, rear seating, and storage.
  9. Resale potential: reliability and brand perception influence value retention.
  10. Finance options: compare rates, deposits, and repayment flexibility.
